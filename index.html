Travel

We are staying at the Hampton Inn at 1751 NE Douglas Street in Lee's Summit, Missouri.

Group pricing is as follows:

  • 2 Queen room for $96
  • 1 King room for $96

Book you room online or over the phone using the group code Hemesath/Hus Wedding. Your room must be booked by September 1st to secure these discounted rates.

Shuttle

A free shuttle will provide transportation between the Hampton Inn and Unity Village. Shuttle times are as follows:

  • 3:00 to 4:30 pm leaving every 15 min.
  • 9:30 to 11:00 pm leaving every 15 min.
